The Crisis

Our Oceans Are Dying 🌊

Every minute, a garbage truck's worth of plastic enters our oceans. Oil spills destroy entire ecosystems. Authorities often find out too late — when the damage is already done.

🛢️

Oil Spills

Oil spills coat marine animals, destroy coral reefs, and contaminate entire coastlines. One spill can devastate an ecosystem for decades.

🧴

Plastic Waste

8 million tonnes of plastic enter our oceans yearly. Microplastics now found in fish, water, and even human blood.

🏭

Industrial Waste

Toxic chemicals from factories poison marine food chains, killing fish and making seafood dangerous for human consumption.

⏰

Delayed Response

By the time authorities discover pollution, it has already spread. Early detection is the key to saving marine ecosystems.

🐠

Marine Life Loss

100,000+ marine mammals and seabirds die annually from pollution. Coral reefs are bleaching at unprecedented rates.

🎣

Human Impact

3 billion people depend on seafood. Polluted oceans threaten food security, livelihoods, and coastal communities worldwide.
